🎯 About Our Mission

Preserving Indonesia's Spice Heritage Through AI Innovation

Our Vision

Indonesia, known as the "Spice Islands," has invaluable spice wealth. We believe artificial intelligence technology can become a bridge between ancestral culinary traditions and today’s digital generation.

Our Mission

To develop an AI-based spice detection system that is accurate, easy to use, and accessible to everyone. We are committed to preserving knowledge about Indonesian spices while helping society recognize and understand the nation’s natural wealth.

Deep Learning Architecture

Our system uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) trained with thousands of Indonesian spice images. This deep learning model can recognize complex visual patterns and provide predictions with up to 95% accuracy.
